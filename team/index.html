About Us

我们是中南大学计算机学院的本科生科研团队，主要成员包括唐健恒、韩非江、范珂嘉、谢文轩等。我们致力于群智感知、众包、深度学习、隐私保护和区块链等前沿技术的研究。

我们在这些领域发表了多篇重要的期刊论文，其中代表性的论文包括：
(2023) Credit and Quality Intelligent Learning based Multi-armed Bandit Scheme for Unknown Worker Selection in Multimedia MCS.
(Information Sciences，中科院一区Top，影响因子8.1)
(2023) BTV-CMAB: A Bi-directional Trust Verification Based Combinatorial Multi-Armed Bandit Scheme for Mobile Crowdsourcing.
(IEEE Internet of Things Journal，中科院一区TopJ，影响因子10.238)
(2023) A Semi-supervised Sensing Rate Learning based CMAB Scheme to Combat COVID-19 by Trustful Data Collection in the Crowd.
(Computer Communications，CR一区，影响因子6.0)
(2023) Minority is all you need: Eliciting the Minority Report in Crowdsourcing.
(AAAI 在投)
(2023) MAB-RP: A Multi-Armed Bandit based Scheme for Accurate Data Collection in Crowdsensing.
(Information Sciences，中科院一区Top，影响因子8.1)
通过这些论文的发表，我们在相关领域取得了显著的研究成果，并为学术界和工业界提供了有价值的贡献。作为团队，我们将继续努力推动科学研究的前沿，并致力于解决现实世界中的问题。
